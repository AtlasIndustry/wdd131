Site Name: Local Community Garden

Site Purpose:

The website will provide information about the garden's history, current projects, events, volunteer opportunities, and gardening tips. It will also feature a photo gallery and a contact form for inquiries or newsletter sign-up.

Scenarios:

What are the upcoming events at the community garden?

How can I volunteer for garden projects?
